### Summary of Findings
Currently, Isekai Castle Crusaders is very limited in terms of gameplay. Play testers don't feel a sense of reward, nor do they feel like they have control over their gameplay. One of the biggest factors to this is the AI for both minions and towers. Towers do not target minions properly, and their bullets deal no damage to minions. Meanwhile, minions can solo the level while the player sits on the sideline.
